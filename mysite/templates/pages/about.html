About Me


Gurpreet Singh has been involved in running businesses since his teenage years. With a background in finance and accounting, Gurpreet received his Real Estate license in 2007 and, for almost 6 years, worked at Fresno Income Properties selling multifamily apartments and small businesses under the supervision of Paul Gestic. While working at Fresno Income Properties, Paul and Gurpreet started a Property Management company, which has become one of the best property management company in Central Valley.

In 2013 Gurpreet moved to Portland, OR and purchasing several small businesses. He successfully ran those businesses until 2018 - subsequently gaining all-around experience running Quick Service Restaurants, (Subways, Sandwich shops, etc), Convenience Stores / Gas Station, Hotels and Motels.

Gurpreet sold these assets in 2018 to start Oregon Business Sales LLC, which specializes in selling and acquisitions of Gas Stations, Quick Service Restaurants, Pizza shops, Liquor Stores Bars.
